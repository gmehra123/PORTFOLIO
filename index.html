Managing Attrition Introduction to survival analysis

In this blog post I talk about survival models that provide a very powerful way to model and study time to event. The event can be a machine failure, loss of a customer (churn) or in the case of medical studies death after diagnosis. In particular I introduce Kaplan Meier curves that are non-parametric curves that help us explore the difference in survival between groups without assuming any distribution or parameters.

Business Imapct:Kaplan-Meier curves help businesses understand how long customers or products last before an event like churn or failure occurs. By revealing patterns in customer retention or product reliability, companies can make informed decisions to improve customer loyalty,increase lifetime value or optimize maintenance strategies.
